  • What are Opioids?

    Opioids are synthetic prescription pills designed as a painkiller substitute for potent drugs like Morphine.

    These synthetic painkillers are highly addictive and often overprescribed by doctors to unknowing patients.

    Despite your chances of being addicted rising to over 90% after just 5 days, opioids are readily prescribed for periods of weeks to a month.
